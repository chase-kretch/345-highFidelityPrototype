OUR MISSION

Share

We hope that users will be able to share their unused things such as old your old baby gear or that deck chair that never gets used to others for free rather than throwing them out and causing unnecessary harm to the environment.

Swap

For those who'd like to get a little something back for there unused valuables and refresh it was something new, swap! Our website both allows and encourages swaps to find the most use of our old things!

Sustain

By joining us you are also joining our movement to reduce waste and sustain our world. Not all things are recyclable but that does not mean they need to reach the landfill. Sign up today and help perserve the world of the future!
